Account Related Queries

How can I change my Click&Cart account password?

To change your Click&Cart account password, follow these steps:

  1. Go to 'My Account'.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Change Password.
  3. Enter your old and new passwords.
  4. Re-enter the new password.
  5. Enter the OTP sent to your mobile number/email ID.
  6. Click on Change Password.

How can I change/update my registered phone number?

To change or update your registered phone number, follow these steps:

  1. Go to 'My Account'.
  2. Under Account Settings, select Update Number.
  3. Enter the new mobile number.
  4. An OTP will be sent via SMS to both the new and old phone numbers.
  5. Enter both OTPs along with your password and click on Verify Mobile.
  6. Click on Save Changes.

Note:

  • If you have logged in using an email-only account, the OTP will be sent to your existing email address to complete the process.
  • Ensure access to both old and new phone numbers as OTPs will be sent to both.
  • If you do not have access to your old number, you won't be able to update the phone number for your account.

How can I change/update my registered email address?

To change or update your registered email address, follow these steps:

  1. Go to 'My Account'.
  2. Under Settings, select Update Email.
  3. Enter your new email ID.
  4. OTPs will be sent to both the existing and new email addresses.
  5. Enter the OTPs along with your password.
  6. Click on Submit/Save Changes.

Note:

  • If you have logged in via a mobile-only account, the OTP will be sent to your existing phone number to complete the update.
  • Ensure access to both old and new email addresses for OTP verification.
  • If you do not have access to your old email address, you won't be able to update the email address for your account.

How can I deactivate my Click&Cart account?

To deactivate your Click&Cart account, follow these steps:

  1. Log into your Click&Cart account.
  2. Go to My Account > Settings > Deactivate.
  3. Enter your account password.
  4. Choose Confirm Deactivation.

I'm getting an error message that says, 'You've exceeded the maximum number of attempts to enter correct verification code'. How can I log in to my Click&Cart account now?

If a wrong code is entered for verification more than a specific number of times, this error message will be seen and for security reasons, your account will be blocked for the next 24 hours. To log in, please try again after 24 hours by entering the correct OTP.

How can I change the email ID linked with my Click&Cart account?

To change the email ID, log in to your Click&Cart account and follow these simple steps:

Steps on the app:

  1. Go to 'Accounts'
  2. Scroll down to 'Edit Profile'
  3. Add the new email ID
  4. Enter the OTPs received on your old & new email ID
  5. Tap 'Save'

Steps on the website:

  1. Visit 'My Profile'
  2. In the 'Personal Information' section, click the 'Edit' button next to the email ID option
  3. Add the new email ID
  4. Enter the OTPs received on your old & new email ID
  5. Click 'Save'

Note: You will not be able to update your email ID without access to your old email ID.
